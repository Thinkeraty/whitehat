Inspired by the ideals of Sardar Patel,

the Gujarat Education Society established,

in 1958, Sardar Patel Vidyalaya to meet the diverse educational needs of the Capital of independent India.

The Gujarat Education Society which established the Vidyalaya manages the Vidyalaya through elected representatives in the Governing Body.

The Governing Body lays down the direction, the policies and the guidelines for the Vidyalaya and monitors its performance on an on going basis.

My Experience:




Hello!
My name is Samyak Jain.
I have been a student at Sardar Patel Vidyalaya for almost 11 years now!
I love this school!
The warmth from the teachers, the fresh air coming from countless trees on the premises and the mesmerizing sight of children studying attract many people!
The academic system is very different from other schools......
It is actually very considerate about students!
They do not give much homework,
They understand that the child needs to have fun too! So they organise fun activities like MEME MAKING, FUNNY POLITICAL DISCUSSIONS etc.
Many times, children do not like coming home!
This school believes in the shaping of the children's character more than the academic scores.
This is one of the oldest and most prestigious schools in the country!
I really like this school!
